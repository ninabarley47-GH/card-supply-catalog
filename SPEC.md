# Card Supply Catalog

## Product Specification

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Frozen

---

## Product Vision

Card Supply Catalog is an offline-first application that helps paper crafters organize, browse, and rediscover their Designer Series Paper (DSP) collection.

The application is intentionally designed as a visual library rather than an inventory management system. Its purpose is to help users quickly recognize papers, find inspiration, and determine whether a paper is still available for use.

Version 1.0 focuses exclusively on Designer Series Paper and emphasizes simplicity, speed, and visual recognition.

Goals

Version 1.0 is designed to allow users to:

Browse their DSP collection visually.
Find papers using partial names, keywords, owners, or release years.
Quickly determine whether a paper is still available.
Add new DSP packs in under one minute (excluding image collection).
View every pattern within a DSP pack on a single screen.
Gradually improve their catalog by adding or replacing images over time.
Share a single catalog among multiple family members while identifying ownership of each paper pack.

The application prioritizes ease of use over exhaustive data collection.

Non-Goals

Version 1.0 intentionally does not include:

Quantity tracking
Purchase price tracking
Manufacturer information
SKU management
Collection management
Storage location tracking
Statistics and reporting
Automatic internet image downloads
Cloud synchronization
User accounts
Favorites
Notes

These features may be considered for future versions but are outside the scope of Version 1.0.
